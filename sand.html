About This Project

Experience realistic particle physics with this advanced sand and water simulator. Watch materials interact according to their physical properties with enhanced water physics.

Advanced Physics Simulation

Experience realistic sand, water, and stone physics with enhanced particle behavior, fluid dynamics, and natural interactions. Watch as sand absorbs water and changes color, stones create impacts, and water flows with realistic splashes.

Sand Physics

Granular physics with wetness absorption, angle of repose, and compaction behavior.

Water Physics

Enhanced fluid dynamics with splashes, pressure simulation, and absorption by sand.

Stone Physics

Solid objects that fall with gravity, displace water, and create impressions in sand.

Advanced Physics Features

  • Sand: Absorbs water and changes color, forms stable piles, and compacts under pressure.
  • Water: Features splashes when hitting surfaces, pressure-based flow, and partial absorption by sand.
  • Stone: Falls with gravity until it hits a surface, displaces water, and creates impressions in sand.
  • Interactions: Sand partially absorbs water, water erodes sand particles, stones displace both materials.
  • Visual Effects: Water splashes, wet sand gradients, and improved particle rendering.

How to Use

  1. Select Sand, Water, or Stone from the material buttons
  2. Choose your brush size from the dropdown
  3. Click and drag to place particles
  4. Use the eraser to remove particles
  5. Clear All resets the entire simulation
  6. Try dropping stones into water or creating sand dams!

Keyboard Shortcuts

S: Sand | W: Water | T: Stone | E: Eraser | C: Clear All

Pro Tip

Drop stones from height to see realistic falling and splash effects!
